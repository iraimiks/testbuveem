AT Homes founder, Alex Tsymbal, was raised on the belief that whatever you do, you need to do it right. You can see and feel this value in each step of our home building process. Every home we build, even after we pass along the keys and move the family in, is our home. Even after the warranty is up, we till take pride in our workmanship and take care of our people.

AT Homes grew from AT Framing, one of the best framing businesses in Columbia, MO, to an up and coming home building company. After 6 years of laying solid frames for homes of other builders, we built our first full home in 2013. We’ve continued down the path to quality home construction since then, selling our homes nearly as quickly as we can build them.
